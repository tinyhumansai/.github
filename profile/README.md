# Welcome to Tiny Humans AI

TinyHumans is an AI lab focused on creating AI algorithims that get us closer to artificial consciousness. See our work

- [Neocortex](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/neocortex) - ([Paper](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/neocortex/tree/main/paper)) A LLM layer with built-in memory to understand large amounts of context and produce artificial consciousness.
- [OpenHuman](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/OpenHuman) - An opensource (GNU) agent that uses the NeoCortex model to simulate a live subsconscious on human data.
- [OpenHuman Skills](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/alphahuman-skills) - An opensource (GNU) registry of powerful skills written for OpenHuman to fetch realtime updates.

## Open-Source Products

Here are a bunch of free open-source products that you can use to build features on top of the Neocortex memory layer. All products are open-sourced under the MIT license.

- [Virtual Call Assistant](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/example-virtual-call-assistant) (WIP) - Similar to Cluely, an open source implementation of a real-time video assistant that is extremly aware about your meeting.
- [Meeting Research Assistant](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/example-meeting-research) (WIP) - An assistnat that lets you research a person who you're about to meet. Useful for investor meetings or sale pitches.
- [Agentic Employee](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/example-agentic-employee) (WIP) - An agentic employee that works for you based on all the information about your company.
- [E-commerce Brain](https://github.com/tinyhumansai/example-ecommerce-brain) (WIP) - An e-commerce assistnat that helps you manage your ecommerce store end-to-end.
